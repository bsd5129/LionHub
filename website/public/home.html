Our Goal

LionHub is a tool which brings together everything a new student needs to be successful. College can be overwhelming, especially for students leaving home for the first time. There is so much information located in so many different places that it can be hard to keep track of everything. Our goal is to provide students with a central location that can direct them to everything they need to succeed.

Overview of Features

Services

Need to drop a class? Want to see if your midterm grade has been posted? LionHub brings all of these important tools quickly to your fingertips.

Favorites

Are any of those services so useful that you need instant access? Add them to your Favorites and they are just one click away.

Rooms

Have a class in a new room? Find a brief description of the room and some general directions of how to get there.

Events

Have a few moments free from studying? Take a look at the Events Calendar and see what is going on around campus.

Scavenger Hunt

Do you want to learn more about campus? Go on a Scavenger Hunt that takes you through campus learning about much of what it has to offer.

Where Am I?

Need to know the name of the building next to you? "Where am I?" brings up information on that building for you.
